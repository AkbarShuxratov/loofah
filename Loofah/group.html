Our Research Group

We are the EcoScrub Research Collective, a dedicated team exploring sustainable bathing solutions. Our current focus is a comparative study of loofahs versus sponges, analyzing their environmental impact, exfoliation efficacy, and hygiene. Founded in 2025, our mission is to inform eco-conscious choices through rigorous science.
